Licq Mailing Lists

There are five mailing lists for Licq announcements and discussion. For more information, including how to subscribe/unsubscribe, and an archive of past posts, follow the relevant link below.

The mail addresses for the lists are list-name@googlegroups.com (e.g. licq-users@googlegroups.com).

How to unsubscribe?

licq-announce

A very low traffic list with announcements about Licq, mostly new releases.

http://groups.google.com/group/licq-announce

Gmane archive

licq-users

Mailing list for Licq users. General discussions and help.

http://groups.google.com/group/licq-users

Gmane archive

licq-dev

The Licq development list. Discussions about the Licq GIT repo, patches and general development related matters.

http://groups.google.com/group/licq-dev

Gmane archive

The Mail Archive

licq-commits

All Licq SVN commits will be posted to this list, allowing developers to keep track of who's changing what.

http://groups.google.com/group/licq-commits

Gmane archive

The Mail Archive

licq-tickets

A fairly high traffic list, mainly for developers. Every time a ticket is added or modified, a message is sent to this list outlining the changes.

http://groups.google.com/group/licq-tickets
